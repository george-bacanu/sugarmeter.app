Introduction

Sugar Meter is a diabetes management app that helps you track glucose readings, insulin doses, and meals. This Privacy Policy explains exactly how your data is handled.

What Data You Enter

The app only stores what you manually enter:

  • Glucose readings (value, tag, notes, time)
  • Insulin doses (units, type, time)
  • Meals (type, carbs, calories)
  • Appointments and reminders
  • Your preferences (units, target range, theme)

We don't automatically collect anything ‚Äî no location, no usage tracking, no device information.

Local Storage

All your data is stored on your device using a local database called Hive. It never leaves your phone unless you choose to export or backup.

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup:

  • Your data is saved to YOUR personal Google Drive
  • It's stored as a JSON file in a SugarMeter folder
  • We cannot access your Google Drive
  • You can delete it anytime
